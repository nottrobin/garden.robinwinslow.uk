A "map of me" is a map of your personality trains and characteristics - your  strengths, virtues, values and passions.

Describing yourself deeply in this way can help others to understand how to work with you, and help you understand what opportunities to persue.

From [Mapping Your Inner World](https://www.lifemaphq.com/articles/mapping-your-inner-world-personality-strengths):

> The Big Five personality traits—Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism—offer a scientific framework for understanding your intrinsic disposition.
